# RollOrNot

**AI-Powered Study Companion — Learn Smarter, Not Harder**

RollOrNot is an iOS app that helps you study smarter. Simply snap a photo of any homework or exam question, and get instant AI-powered analysis with detailed explanations and personalized practice.
